🔒 Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy – English

Last updated: 2026

Our applications (Snooker Counter TD and Shoot-Out & Pool Timer) do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All game statistics, scores, and timer settings are stored exclusively on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. No internet permissions are required for core functionality.

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact us at oreanodse@gmail.com.
